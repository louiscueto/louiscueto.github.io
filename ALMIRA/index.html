An Overview

Well-placed in the beaches, sightseeing, family fun area of Bohol city, Almira Diving Resort provides a most conducive spot for you to take a break from your busy days. The city center is merely 10 km away and the airport can be reached within 20 minutes. A well-kempt environment and its proximity to Momo Beach, Bingag Cave, The Philippine Tarsier Foundation give to this hotel a special charm.
